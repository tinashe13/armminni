    Our story

    Armminni is a new vibrant brand in the Zimbabwean Market . We specialise in the supply of corporate wear, school wear and sports wear.

    Already we have made an impact by providing high quality and professionally made corporate wear. We also distribute known such as US Basic . Slazenger, Biz Collection , Elevate ,Gary Player and Cutter and Buck

    Our Approach

    • We allow you to concentrate on your core business whilst we ensure that your employees / students are dressed professionally and comfortably
    • We give you a wide selection to choose from both in terms of colour and design

    • We guarantee you high quality - all our clothes are made to last and with the best fabrics to ensure longetivity and best fit
    • All garments are delivered with in the agreed time frames
    • We give you value for money
